Thanks for stopping by! I'm Sarah Symphetamine. A little about me: a Miami native that loves to travel and currently resides in Gainesville, FL. I've lived in Los Angeles, Philadelphia, Canada, Peru & Costa Rica. I'm a well traveled plus-sized queen, who loves all things music and art. I grew up singing in choir and playing piano all throughout high school. I also have my Associate in Science in Private Investigation. I've worked in different fields of law (family, real estate, workers compensation and traffic). I also recently worked in dialysis, so when I say I'm a jack of all trades, I mean it. I am CPR certified, a notary and ordained. But really, my passion is all things music and art. I decided to get into the field of graphic design so I can make album covers for potential artists and work with musicians to help them achieve the art they want their fans to see when they release new music. I am an avid vinyl collector - I have roughly 300 vinyls currently. My collection ranges, but I thoroughly enjoy finding first editions. Some of those are:

  • Star Wars Episode Four Soundtrack
  • Queen - Jazz
  • The Beach Boys - Pet Sounds
  • The Beatles - Introducing...The Beatles
  • Fleetwood Mac - Rumors

Hopefully, we can bond over music and creative ideas to have your vision come to life in the form of a cover. I look forward to working with you!
